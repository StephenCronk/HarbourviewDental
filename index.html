Frequently Asked

Questions

What services does your clinic offer?

We offer comprehensive care at Harbourview Dental Studios, including routine checkups and cleanings, restorations (fillings), crown and bridge, root canals, and dentures. We also offer specialized care including implant placement and restoration, as well as periodontal treatment.

How do I schedule an appointment with Harbourview Dental Studios?

Feel free to give us a call anytime at 604-555-8231 or using the webform below.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans including the CDCP.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a government-issued ID, any insurance cards, and your medical records and a list of any prescription medications you are taking.
